About

How this is built

All maps use official boundary data from the ONS Geography Portal and the UK Data Service, converted to TopoJSON and rendered with D3.js. Election results come from the House of Commons Library.

Boundaries are matched to their correct era — 1983 and 1987 use the 1983 boundary set, 2010 through 2019 use the 2010 set, and so on. Constituency names are normalised across boundary eras to ensure consistent matching.

This is a personal project. If you find errors or have suggestions, please get in touch.
